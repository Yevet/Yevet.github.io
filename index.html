Research and Implementation of Activity Recognition Algorithm Based on Deep Learning

Research Project & Graduation Design

Deep learning has become relatively popular nowadays. Scholars begin to devote themselves to applying deep learning in various aspects, and the application of deep learning in the field of human activity recognition(HAR) also appears. This paper summarizes the shortcomings of previous researches in the field of activity recognition, and proposes a new network, DeepHAR network, on the basis of simple LSTM. The new network can ensure the effectiveness of information transmission by adding residual connections (in depth dimension) and bidirectional units (in time dimension). In this case, the bottom information can be transmitted from bottom to bottom, avoiding gradient disappearance problem. Finally, the model’s performance can be improved. Also, the performance of different networks in different data sets are compared in detail in the paper. The final network achieves 86.69% and 93.34% accuracy in self-built smartwatch data set and public UCI HAR data set. The paper also introduces a smartwatch sensor data collection system that can be used on Ticwatch Pro 3. Through this system, data changes of gyroscope and accelerometer in the watch can be collected and stored at different frequencies and active labels, which is conducive to future research on activity recognition based on smart watches.
